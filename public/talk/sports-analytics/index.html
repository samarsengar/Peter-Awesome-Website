Data Visualization in Tennis

Slides
Image credit: CBC

Abstract

With Bianca Andreescu becoming the first ever Canadian to hoist a Grand Slam singles trophy, tennis mania has taken over Canada. In this talk, we present some graphics to represent the rising strength of Canadian tennis relative to other competing nations. We also present some interesting patterns in tennis serving tactics observed in recent grand slam tournaments.
